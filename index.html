Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Nur Salah respects your privacy. We do not create user accounts, sell personal data, or use personal data for advertising or tracking.

Data Stored on Your Device

Prayer tracking, mood check-ins, app settings, notification preferences, and similar app data are stored locally on your device. This information is not uploaded to our servers.

Location Usage

The app may use your device location to calculate prayer times and help identify the appropriate local prayer-time schedule. Location data is processed on your device and is not sent to our prayer-time storage service.

Official Prayer-Time Schedules

To provide official local prayer schedules for supported regions, the app may download public schedule files from Supabase Storage, a cloud file-hosting service.

These files contain prayer-time schedules only. The app does not send your name, email address, prayer history, mood check-ins, precise location, or other profile information to Supabase.

Like most online services, the hosting provider may process limited technical connection information, such as IP address and request logs, in order to deliver the requested files securely and reliably.

Notifications

The app may use notifications to remind users about prayer times. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Nur Salah does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app or its features change. The latest version will be available through the Privacy Policy link associated with the app.

Contact
