Privacy Policy

Nur Salah respects your privacy. This app does not collect or share personal data with external servers.

Data Storage

All data (such as prayer tracking and mood check-ins) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Location Usage

The app may use your device location to calculate accurate prayer times. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared.

Notifications

The app may use notifications to remind users about prayer times.
